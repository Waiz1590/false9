OFFICIAL RULEBOOK

Version 1.0 // The essentials — read before you register

01

Tournament Format

Format depends on the number of registered teams — Knockout, League + Knockout, Round Robin, or Group Stage + Knockout. Always announced before registrations open.

02

Team Composition

5-a-side: 5 on field, squad of up to 8, minimum 4 to start. 7-a-side: 7 on field, squad of up to 10, minimum 5 to start. A player can only represent one team per tournament.

03

Reporting Time

All teams must report 30 minutes before their first match unless stated otherwise. Late arrivals risk a walkover.

04

Substitutions

Rolling substitutions are allowed throughout the match. Players may only enter or leave the field when the referee permits or play has stopped.

05

Sliding Tackles

5-a-side: not permitted (goalkeepers exempt inside their own area). 7-a-side: depends on surface and will be announced beforehand. Dangerous or reckless tackles are always penalised.

06

Player Conduct

No yellow or red cards — False9ine runs a 3-warning system. Three warnings in a match and you're removed from it. Serious misconduct can mean immediate removal from the tournament, no warning needed.

07

Walkovers

A team forfeits if they fail to report on time, don't have the minimum players, or refuse to continue. Referee and organisers decide the result.

08

Weather & Delays

Matches may be delayed, suspended, or rescheduled for heavy rain or unsafe conditions. Any changes will be communicated as early as possible.

09

Refunds & Liability

Registration fees are generally non-refundable once confirmed, except if False9ine cancels the tournament. All participants play at their own risk.
